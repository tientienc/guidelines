The LifeQ logomark

A logomark is a symbol or icon that represents the brand. It is an identifying mark or symbol that doesn’t contain the business name, and can serve as a logo replacement under special circumstances. In our case, it is the LifeQ tree.

The HealthQ logomark

LifeQ logo usage

  • The LifeQ logo must be placed on EVERY touchpoint.

LifeQ logomark usage

LifeQ Tree and the HealthQ Q

  • Used in mobile and desktop applications.
  • Used in animations.
  • Used as logomark on business card.

Logo color usage

General color usage

Primary usage (#103140)

  • Logo should always be LifeQ Dark Blue.

Secondary usage (#A2AEBB)

  • Used in email touchpoints when logo is not the primary viewpoint.
  • Used in the header of documentation (once the LifeQ Dark Blue logo has been placed on the cover page).

Exceptions

  • Use the LifeQ White logo when the logo needs to be placed on a dark background.
  • You may only use the LifeQ Black logo when the touchpoint demands a strict black and white design.

Color usage for placement of logo on backgrounds and images

Logo must be readable and have significant contrast with the image or background.

You may only use the LifeQ Dark Blue logo on top of lighter colors and white.

You may only use the LifeQ Gray logo on top of white backgrounds (exceptions may apply).
Do not place the LifeQ Gray logo on top of a LifeQ Dark Blue background.

Logo placement and scaling

The "e" distance

  • When placed next to objects, text, colors, etc., the padding should be applied (i.e. the box that goes with the logo should not be touched, overlapped, removed, or adjusted).
  • Always keep width of “e” around logo.
  • Padding around the logo should be 20% of the full size.
  • Minimum size
    • Digital: Logo may not be smaller than 24 px wide.
    • Print: Logo may not be smaller than .5 in (12.7 mm) wide.

Placement regulation

Backgrounds

Pattern
  • Logo may not be placed on a pattern that steals the attention from the logo. Find a different image or consult the design team for instructions.
  • Logo may not be placed in a random box on top of a pattern. It is not a sticker!
Banner
  • Logo may never be placed in the center of a social media banner.
  • Logo must be placed on the left or right side and may NOT be obstructed by a profile photo (like on Facebook, Linkedin or Twitter).
  • Logo must be at 100% opacity.

Illustrations

This includes icons, duos, and images, i.e. infographics.

  • The LifeQ logo plays a supporting role in all our visual elements. It is never in the center, bolder, or the only image within that illustration. It is a team player, making the other visual elements the main attraction and itself the supporter and proud friend.
  • As the other visual elements are meant to be the star of the show, the logo should play the supporting role by sitting in the bottom corner on the left or right, at 56% opacity.
  • While the logo is never the star of the show, it sometimes plays an equal role to the other visual elements. In this case, it may be as big as the other elements and at 100% opacity.
  • When placing the logo and an icon together, it should be placed above or below the icon.
  • The logo must NEVER be squished to be placed inside the chosen illustration. It may ONLY be placed inside an icon, if there is the "e" distance between the logo and the icon line edges.

Photography

This includes original photography and stock images.

  • The logo must NEVER be placed in the center of a photograph.
  • The logo can only be in the bottom left or right corner of an image.
  • It needs to be in the least disruptive corner of the photograph.

Animations

  • The logo or logomark must be featured at the start of an animation as a form of introduction.
  • The logo must always be featured in the beginning or at the end of an animation as a form of signature.
  • When commercialized, or posted online, an animation MUST have copyright and registered trademark symbols on it.

Logo Do's and Don'ts

You may only use the LifeQ White logo on LifeQ Dark Blue and LifeQ Blue backgrounds.

DO NOT use the LifeQ Blue logo on the LifeQ Dark Blue background.

Do not add any company names to the LifeQ logo, e.g. LifeQ Global, LifeQ Labs, etc. The logo must ALWAYS be just LifeQ.

Any addition should live outside scaling padding.

Using the company name in writing

Always capitalize the “L” and "Q" in LifeQ.
Always write out the name LifeQ.

LifeQ

lifeq, LQ, Lifeq, lifeQ

Do not use the possessive form of LifeQ, i.e. "LifeQ's".

e.g.: "...part of the LifeQ ecosystem."

e.g.: "...part of LifeQ's ecosystem."
