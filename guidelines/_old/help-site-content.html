Guidelines & Assets

Help Site Copy

Basics

Before you read over this page, please review the Copy Style Guide and familiarize yourself with it.

Additional guidelines

Tone

  1. Credible, informative and helpful.

Length

  1. Keep instructions as brief and simple as possible.
  2. Keep in mind, however, that clarity takes precedence over brevity.

Numerals

  1. Always write out one to nine, and use numerals from 10 onwards.
  2. Exception: always use numerals with measuring units.

Uppercase vs. lowercase

  1. Use sentence case for headings (only first word and proper nouns capitalized).

Referring to on-screen text

  1. When referring to buttons, instructions, options and pop-up messages that appear in the app or on the tracker, write it exactly as it appears on screen (uppercase/lowercase) and place it in bold.

Referring to other sections of the Help Site

  1. When referring user to different section of Help Site, hyperlink the name of section.
  2. When referring to part of a section, place it in quotation marks (e.g. See "Your tracker" in the FAQs section).
