Guidelines & Assets

Social Media

So much content is shared in and around the Q’Niverse, but we want the rest of the world to know and interact with us, too! Understandably, we hold a lot of confidential information, but we also have a lot that we can and want to share with everyone else. Social media platforms provide ways for the Q’Niverse to share our voice with others, while also giving our employees the opportunity to share their thoughts and musings on LifeQ and HealthQ.

Posting Conditions

When using Gregor Röhrig’s photography on Twitter or Instagram, you MUST credit his social media name @gregorsnaps. When posting Gregor Röhrig’s photography anywhere else, you MUST credit his full name.

If there is any doubt over whether a photograph contains sensitive information, ensure that someone from executive management signs off on it (especially when it comes to photos with whiteboards in the background). We can simply blur out any confidential information if we do not get a response from management.

Do not slander, misuse, demean, or harm LifeQ in any way when posting content associated with its name on social media. Stay respectful, cool, and NDA trustworthy.
