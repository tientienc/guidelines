Guidelines & Assets

Photography

Photos are used to narrate our company’s story and to show off our incredible team, the spaces we work in and the magic that happens behind the scenes. It can be used for press releases, presentations, social media and to decorate our offices around the world.

Usage

  • Visual story
  • Press release imagery
  • Presentations
  • Newsletter
  • Merchandise
  • Conventions

Gregor Röhrig Photography / Definitive Process

If you see a guy with a camera at the office, don’t be alarmed. Gregor has been with the company since the beginning, visually capturing our story and journey. He is a visual anthropologist whose mission is to capture important, memorable and every day moments of the business. Over the past five years he has created an archive of visual material that allows us to see and understand the progress and behind-the-scenes workings of this company and its culture. Feel free to chat to him; the more you share your team’s workings with him, the better he'll be able to document it.

Gregor calls his way of working the definitive process. Through this process, he uses visual storytelling to create a lasting record of events, provide valuable insight, retain memories, observe culture, increase transparency, promote productivity, and ignite inspiration.

  • http://www.gregorrohrig.com/definitive-process
  • @gregorsnaps

External image use

  • Because of the sensitive nature of the images, with the risk of IP being exposed, any images used for external purposes HAVE to be approved by a team lead whose work or team is being featured in the photo.
  • If images are requested by any commercial third party, the image needs to be licensed with a commercial agreement through Gregor Röhrig.

Logo placement

  • Gregor’s logo must be placed on all his photos going into the outside world.
  • When in a set, it needs to be placed on the first or last image of the set.
  • If not placed on the image (given it was cropped or people do not listen) his name MUST be credited on the corresponding document or platform.

Copyright rules

  • Gregor Röhrig owns the rights to all his photographs.

Social media use

  • If his logo is not on the image, use social media handle.

Press release usage

  • Logo MUST be on image.
  • Name MUST be credited in the article.

Stock Photography

Stock photography may be used as well, but may ONLY be used for press releases. It is only to be used when the subject matter does NOT fall under the categories of technology, software/comp, or office culture. If the content falls under one of the mentioned categories, Gregor's photography is to be used.

If you need new stock imagery, please contact the Creative team.
