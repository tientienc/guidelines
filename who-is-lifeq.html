Who is LifeQ?

At LifeQ, we want to make optimal health accessible and affordable for all people. We aim to inspire our team members and communities alike to lead healthier lives, and to provide them with the tools they need to improve their wellness. Our brand is credible, informative and friendly, and rather than selling products and closing deals, we work to earn people’s trust and loyalty.

Why we are

Guided by the above mission, we are dedicated to finding digital health and wellness solutions that combine innovation and scalability.

A quote that is very close to the heart of Riaan Conradie, our company’s president and one of its co-founders, is from the 1948 constitution of the World Health Organization (WHO). They define health as “a state of complete physical, mental and social well-being and not merely the absence of disease or infirmity”.

How

Our competitive advantage is health innovation scaled. We gather a large amount of physiological data from a multitude of tracking devices and deliver it to our specialist team of scientists, tech wizards, engineers and mathematicians who transform it into valuable health insights. These insights, which would otherwise only have been obtainable through expensive, time-consuming and invasive procedures, are customized to meet the needs of various business sectors. It is this tailored approach to healthcare solutions that sets us apart.

Making our why a reality

We have a variety of touchpoints to help us achieve our goals, including emails, apps, press releases, data, merchandise, etc.

Together our WHY and HOW define WHO we are.

If LifeQ were a person

The three facets of LifeQ

1

Scientist
(peer-facing)

LifeQ is an industry-leading scientist who is curious about how the human body works and how our lifestyles affect our health. The main ambition is to make optimal healthcare accessible and affordable for all.

2

Business partner
(customer-facing)

LifeQ believes in the power of collaboration and aims to leverage key partnerships to disrupt the healthcare industry. LifeQ is a strategic forward-thinker who always keeps the bigger picture in mind.

3

Human being
(user- and participant-facing)

LifeQ is friendly and eager to share valuable knowledge with people from all walks of life. LifeQ is the smartest person in the room, but also the most approachable. LifeQ will listen to your questions, fully aware that you may not have a scientific background, and answer in a way that is easy to understand.

LifeQ is a human being first and a scientist second.
